About Me

Professional healthcare provider serving Bangladesh with dedication and expertise

As a Registered Nurse with specialized training in rehabilitation and pressure sore management, I bring a unique combination of clinical expertise and compassionate care to every patient interaction. My journey in healthcare has been defined by a commitment to excellence and continuous learning.


Currently serving through government healthcare facilities and specialized rehabilitation services at the Centre for the Rehabilitation of the Paralysed (CRP), I focus on providing comprehensive care to patients with complex needs, particularly those with mobility limitations and neurological conditions.


My approach to nursing emphasizes evidence-based practice, patient education, and interdisciplinary collaboration to achieve optimal health outcomes. I believe in treating not just the condition, but the whole person, ensuring dignity and quality of life for every patient under my care.
